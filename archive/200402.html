Ice Ice Baby

By Martin Woodward on February 23, 2004 8:05 PM

Got given this fantastic book by my wife. It is a very interesting account of the trade in ice from America to rest of the world. This explains why the British are soo stingy when it comes to ice in drinks and why every fridge in America has an ice machine and why us brits like warm flat bitter instead of ice cold largers. Doesn't explain all you can drink soda's though. Well worth a read.

IDEA 4.0 Rocks

By Martin Woodward on February 2, 2004 11:24 PM

Been using the Beta version of Intellij 4.0 on my last project. It is now on late release candidate builds and is fantastic. Previously I was an Eclipse fan, however this version has me sold.

Haven't tried out the generics support for Java 1.5 yet, but I've heard so many people talking about it I think I may have to see what the fuss is about.

Brian Coyner seems to agree with me in his review O'Reilly Network: IDEA 4.0 - Is It Worth The Money? [Jan. 23, 2004]
